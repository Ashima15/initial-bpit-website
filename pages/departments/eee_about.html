About this department

Electrical energy is one of the most important inventions of the last century. As the electrical energy is one of the most important requirements of one and all in whole of the world, its growing demand warrants new innovations for enhanced production, efficient transmission and management. These require quality engineers, therefore, the department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering is grooming the youth through class-room teaching, laboratory practices and field visits to take new challenges in the area of electrical & electronics engineering.
